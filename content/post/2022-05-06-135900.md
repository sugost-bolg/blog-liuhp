\n落地服务器\n\n
\n```\n`nohup ./gost -L relay+tls://:50505/:9050 >> /dev/null 2>&1 &
`\n```\n
\nwindows客户端\n\n
\n```\n`gost >nul 2>nul -L tcp://:10000 -F relay+tls://192.168.100.100:50505
gost >nul 2>nul -L tcp://:20000 -F relay+tls://192.168.100.200:50505
`\n```\n
\nwindows负载均衡客户端\n\n
\n```\n`gost >nul 2>nul -L=:9050 -F=socks5://:10000,:20000&strategy=round&max_fails=1&fail_timeout=30s
`\n```\n
