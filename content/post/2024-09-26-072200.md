\n安装\n\n
\n```\n`apt install shadowsocks-libev
`\n```\n
\n进入配置文件\n\n
\n```\n`cd /etc/shadowsocks-libev
`\n```\n
\n修改配置\n\n
\n```\n`{
    "server":"0.0.0.0",
    "mode":"tcp_and_udp",
    "server_port":8388,
    "local_port":1080,
    "password":"123456",
    "timeout":86400,
    "method":"aes-256-gcm"
}
`\n```\n
\n服务端口 18339， 本地端口 8388，密码 123456，加密方式 aes-256-gcm\n\n
\n重启服务器测试自启动\n\n
