\ncentos7 安装命令\n\n
\n```\n`wget https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MvsCode/frps-onekey/master/install-frps.sh -O ./install-frps.sh
chmod 700 ./install-frps.sh
./install-frps.sh install
`\n```\n
\nUninstall（卸载）\n\n
\n```\n`./install-frps.sh uninstall
`\n```\n
\nUpdate（更新）\n\n
\n```\n`./install-frps.sh update
`\n```\n
\nServer management（服务管理器）\n\n
\n```\n`Usage: /etc/init.d/frps {start|stop|restart|status|config|version}
`\n```\n
\n转发自[Frps服务端一键配置脚本](https://github.com/MvsCode/frps-onekey)\n\n
