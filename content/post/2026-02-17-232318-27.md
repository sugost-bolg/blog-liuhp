\n## 进入目录\n\n\n```\n`cd /usr/share/caddy/`\n```\n\n## 开机自启\n\n\n```\n`cd /usr/share/caddy &&  chmod +x install_log2html_service.sh && ./install_log2html_service.sh`\n```\n\n## 测试log2html\n\n\n```\n`cd /usr/share/caddy && node log2html.js`\n```\n\n## 暂停服务\n\n\n```\n`systemctl stop log2html.service`\n```\n\n## 重启服务\n\n\n```\n`systemctl restart log2html.service`\n```\n\n## 关停服务\n\n\n```\n`systemctl disable log2html.service`\n```\n\n## 查看日志\n\n\n```\n`journalctl -u log2html.service -f`\n```\n\n## 备份log2html\n\n\n```\n`cp /usr/share/caddy/log2html.js /usr/share/caddy/log2html.js.bak`\n```\n\n\n\n
